At the beginning of the experiment you have an endowment of {{ Constants.endowment }}. There is an urn containing 10 balls, either red or green, from which one ball will be drawn. If the drawn ball is red, you will receive €0, if it is green you will receive additional €10. The distribution of red and green balls is not known, but you have the possibility to replace {{ player.number_remove }} balls randomly drawn with green balls.
When you click the next button, you will be asked first, about how many green balls you think the urn contains and second, how much of your {{ Constants.endowment }} you would spend to let the replacement take place. If this number is greater than some randomly chosen price (between €0 and €10) the replacement takes place, if it is smaller, the urn stays unmodified. You will be informed about this result.
After the replacement has or has not taken place, one ball will be drawn and you will be informed about whether you won or lost.
